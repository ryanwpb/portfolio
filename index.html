What I love about Being a Front End Developer.

I love that everyday I get to my desk, dawn my headphones and open my laptop my day is completely different from yesterday. So, who am I as a person?

Well to start, I love to cook! I love food period. I really enjoy entertaining guest and serving them something they may have never tried before. Also I'm a huge fan of music.
